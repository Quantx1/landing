## 1. Important context before you subscribe

Please read this before paying for any plan:

1.1. **What Quant X is.** Quant X provides **informational, analytical, and educational tools only** — ML-driven signal engines, an AI copilot/chat, stock and options screeners, backtesting, and paper trading. Any optional live trading is executed **on your own broker account** (Zerodha / Upstox / Angel One) via broker OAuth, using your own funds and at your own risk.

1.2. **What Quant X is not.** Quant X is **not** registered with SEBI as a Research Analyst (RA) or Investment Adviser (IA), and holds **no** NSE algo empanelment. Nothing on the Platform is investment advice, a recommendation to buy or sell, or a guarantee of returns or profit. We do **not** handle your funds or securities and do **not** offer assured returns or profit-sharing. You make your own trading decisions.

1.3. **This is a subscription for software access — not for outcomes.** Subscription fees pay for access to Platform features for a billing period. They are **not** contingent on any trade being profitable, any signal being correct, or any market outcome. Losses, missed opportunities, or dissatisfaction with signal performance are **not** grounds for a refund.

1.4. **You can evaluate for free before paying.** The Free plan (₹0) and **paper trading with virtual funds** let you assess whether Quant X suits you **before** you pay for any subscription. We encourage you to do so.

## 3. No-refund policy

3.1. **Subscription fees are non-refundable.** Once a billing period has started and payment has been taken, the fee for that billing period is **non-refundable**, whether or not you use the Platform during that period. This applies to both first-time payments and auto-renewals.

3.2. **No pro-rata refunds.** If you cancel, downgrade, or stop using the Platform partway through a billing period, you will **not** receive a pro-rata or partial refund for the unused portion of that period. Instead, your paid access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for (see Section 4).

3.3. **Reasons this policy is fair.** We maintain a no-refund policy because:
   - a free tier (₹0) and paper trading let you evaluate the Platform at no cost before paying;
   - subscriptions are month-to-month, so your maximum commitment is a single month;
   - access to paid features (signals, AI copilot, screeners, backtesting) is delivered immediately and consumed over the period; and
   - fees are for **software access**, not for trading results or market outcomes.

3.4. **This does not override your statutory rights.** Nothing in this Policy limits any right to a refund or remedy that you may have under applicable Indian law, including the Consumer Protection Act, 2019. See Section 5.

## 4. How to cancel

4.1. **You can cancel at any time.** You may cancel your paid subscription at any time, with no cancellation fee, by:
   - using the **Cancel / Manage Subscription** option in your account settings on quantx.app; or
   - emailing **[PLACEHOLDER: billing@quantx.app]** from your registered email address.

4.2. **Access continues until period end.** When you cancel, your subscription remains active and you keep full access to your paid plan **until the end of the current billing period you have already paid for**. Cancellation stops the **next** auto-renewal; it does not cut off access immediately and does not trigger a refund for the current period.

4.3. **After the period ends.** At the end of the paid period, your account reverts to the **Free** plan. Paid-only features become unavailable, but your account, saved data, and paper-trading history remain accessible subject to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

4.4. **Turning off auto-renewal.** Cancelling before the next renewal date is how you turn off auto-renewal. We recommend cancelling at least **[PLACEHOLDER: e.g. 24–48 hours]** before your renewal date to ensure the next charge is not initiated, since recurring charges may be queued in advance under RBI e-mandate rules.

4.5. **Broker disconnection is separate.** Cancelling your subscription does not automatically revoke any broker OAuth connection. You can disconnect your broker at any time from your account settings; doing so is independent of your subscription status.

## 5. Exceptions — when we will issue a refund

Despite the no-refund policy in Section 3, we will review and, where warranted, issue a refund in the following limited situations:

5.1. **Where required by law.** Where a refund is required by applicable Indian law, including the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 and related rules (for example, in cases of a deficiency in service as determined under law). This Policy does not exclude or limit any non-waivable statutory right you have as a consumer.

5.2. **Duplicate or erroneous charges.** If you were **charged more than once** for the same billing period, or charged in error due to a technical or processing fault on our side or the payment gateway's side, we will refund the **duplicate or erroneous amount**.

5.3. **Proven non-delivery / prolonged unavailability.** If you paid for a plan but were **unable to access the paid features you paid for** due to a verified fault attributable to us (for example, a sustained outage that prevents access for a material portion of the billing period), and the issue could not be resolved after you raised it with support, we may issue a full or partial refund at our reasonable discretion. Normal, intermittent, or scheduled maintenance downtime, and issues caused by your own device, network, browser, or third-party broker/data feed, do **not** qualify.

5.4. **What does not qualify.** For clarity, the following are **not** grounds for a refund: dissatisfaction with signal accuracy or backtest / model performance; trading losses or missed gains; a change of mind after paying; forgetting to cancel before an auto-renewal; or non-use of the Platform during a paid period.

5.5. **How approved refunds are paid.** Approved refunds are made to the **original payment method** used for the purchase. Refund timelines depend on your bank / card issuer / payment gateway and are typically **[PLACEHOLDER: e.g. 5–10 business days]** after approval. We do not offer cash refunds and do not operate any separate refund or cashback surface.

5.6. **How to request.** To request a refund under this Section, email **[PLACEHOLDER: billing@quantx.app]** from your registered email with your account details, the transaction ID / date, and a description of the issue. See also the grievance process in Section 8.
